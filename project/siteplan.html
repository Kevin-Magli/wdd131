Athema

The name is sort of a mix of "a theme - um tema" in portuguese and Athena the greek goddess. One of the titles of Athena is "The Goddess of Art" and this entire site will revolve around themes like the one you're seeing now in this page.

Site Purpose

This site will be a collection or a Hub of dynamic themes that can be applied to see how they look and behave. You'll be able to see, experiment and receive a "converted" version of it to many apps and programs. This will be the go-to to find a theme that suits you.

Scenarios - Target Audience

  • What kind of themes are available?
  • How can I get a theme?
  • Where i can find a theme that suits my needs?
